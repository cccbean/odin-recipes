Platano maduro frito

Platano maduro frito (not my photo)

Description

Fried plantains are yummy. Please make.

Ingredients

  • a couple ripe plantains, should have black spots, cut into thick slivers
  • oil for frying
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat up the oil in a skillet until it reads some good temperature for frying...
  2. Fry the plantains a couple at a time until golden brown on both sides, be careful not to overcrowd the skillet.
  3. Once done, place the plantains on a plate lined with paper towels to soak up the excess oil. Be sure to sprinkle with salt while still hot.
  4. Enjoy!
